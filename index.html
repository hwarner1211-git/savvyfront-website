Beyond the Hype: What Real Uptime Looks Like

When you choose hosting, you’re choosing the foundation your business runs on. Most providers offer standard VPS configurations where your service lives on a single physical machine. If that machine has a network card failure, power issue, or disk fault, your service goes down until it's manually restored.

SavvyFront is built on a different philosophy: Redundancy as default.

Feature Standard Hosting (Single Server) SavvyFront (Proxmox/Ceph Cluster)
Hardware Dependency High. One failing drive or PSU means downtime. Low. Services instantly migrate to healthy nodes.
Data Protection Basic RAID, susceptible to failure scenarios. Ceph storage actively mirrors data across multiple independent machines.
Performance Consistency Can suffer during hardware maintenance or neighbor issues. Consistent high performance due to workload balancing across the cluster.
Upgrade Path Requires planned maintenance window and migration downtime. Seamless live migration. We can upgrade hardware components without you ever noticing.

The Bottom Line: We use the same battle-tested, open-source technology that powers massive cloud infrastructures to deliver predictable, robust performance for your business applications.
